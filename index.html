Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days or more before the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations made 15-29 days before the event will receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot provide refunds for cancellations made less than 15 days before the event.

Is there a group discount?

Yes, we offer group discounts for teams of 5 or more people from the same organization. Please contact our registration team at registration@cargopharmaconference.com for more information.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all main stage sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees for 30 days after the conference. Breakout sessions may not all be recorded, so please check the agenda for details.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We are following all local health guidelines and will implement appropriate safety measures. Specific protocols will be communicated to registered attendees closer to the event date.
